Autoresearch v2 demo: baseline, human best, ours best

Presentation snapshot using preserved completed runs. Denominators are shown explicitly, so N=32, N=96, and larger search results can be compared by success percentage without pretending they are identical experiments.

Core message

Each target is reported as Baseline, Human best, and Ours best. CD45 is the clearest positive case: Baseline is 8/32, Human best is 15/32, and Ours best is 22/32 with 5 unique clusters.

Success criterion: a self binder must pass normalized self_complex_i_pAE <= 7/31 (~0.226), self_complex_pLDDT >= 0.9, and self_binder_scRMSD_ca < 1.5. Lower iPAE and scRMSD are better; higher pLDDT is better.
