Autoresearch v2 demo: baseline, human best, ours best

Presentation snapshot using preserved completed runs. Denominators are shown explicitly, so N=32, N=96, and larger search results can be compared by success percentage without pretending they are identical experiments.

Core message

Each target is reported as Baseline, Human best, and Ours best. CD45 is the clearest positive case: Baseline is 8/32, Human best is 15/32, Ours best audited tree node is 22/32 with 5 unique clusters, a larger Ours raw node reaches 86/192, and the fixed rerun reaches 41/96.

Success criterion follows the existing local analysis: iPAE pass, pLDDT pass, and final binder success. Lower iPAE is better; higher pLDDT is better.
